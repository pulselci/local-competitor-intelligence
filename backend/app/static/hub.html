📋 Pulse LCI Playbook

Standard operating procedures for running cold outreach and onboarding new clients.

Phase 1 — Prospect Discovery

1
Run prospect discovery Outreach tool

In the Outreach tab → Discovery section. Enter city + state, pick categories. Discovery runs in the background (~2–5 min). Results land in the queue as draft_ready or no_email.

2
Review the queue Quality check

Open the queue tab in Outreach. Skim business names — skip chains, low-quality prospects, or businesses you already know. Edit the draft email if the auto-generated copy needs tweaking.

3
Approve & send Sends email

Click Approve for prospects with a good email. The system sends the cold email immediately from craig@pulselci.com with the one-sheet PDF attached. Status changes to sent.

Phase 2 — Follow-up Cadence

4
Day-5 follow-up (auto) Cron job

The cron at /cron/send-followups auto-sends a Day-5 nudge to any cold prospect who hasn't replied. Check the Follow-ups tab to see what's pending.

5
Day-12 follow-up (auto) Cron job

A final short bump is sent 12 days after the initial send. After this, no further automated contact. Prospects who reply should be moved to the onboarding flow manually.

6
Mark converted Follow-ups tool

If someone replies and agrees to a free report, click "Convert" in the cold prospects section. This flags them for manual onboarding — proceed to Phase 3.

Phase 3 — Onboarding

7
Fill out the onboarding form Onboarding tool

In the Onboarding tab, enter the business name, city, state, Google Place ID, and contact email. The system creates the business record, ingests competitors, and sets up the report schedule.

8
Generate the first report Generates PDF

Once onboarded, trigger a manual report from the Admin Stats or Clients dashboard. The PDF is generated and emailed to the contact address automatically. Confirm delivery via the Clients tab.

9
Send the Stripe payment link (if converting to paid) Billing

In Admin → Billing, generate a Stripe checkout link for the business. Send it manually by email. Once paid, the schedule flips to enabled and appears in the Active Subscribers section of the Clients dashboard.

Ongoing Operations

10
Monthly reports run automatically Cron job

The /cron/run-scheduled-reports cron fires monthly and generates + emails reports for all active subscribers. Check Admin Stats for last-run timestamps and any failures.

11
Monitor growth alerts Follow-ups tool

Growth alert emails go out when a business's competitors make a notable gain. These are separate from monthly reports — check the Growth Alerts section of Follow-ups.
