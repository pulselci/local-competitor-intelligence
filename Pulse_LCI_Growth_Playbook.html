How the queue actually works — read this first

Each discovery run returns 20–60 raw Google results. After filtering (reviews, rating, chain detection), and after only keeping prospects where an email was found, you typically get 10–30 sendable contacts per run. To send 50 emails a day, you need to run 2–3 discovery batches every week across multiple cities and verticals.

Rule: If your queue drops below 50, run a new discovery batch immediately — don't wait for your scheduled day. Think of the queue like a fuel tank: keep it above half.

5 non-negotiable rules

01

Send every business day — no exceptions. One missed week sets you back two weeks in pipeline.

02

Reply to every inbound within 2 hours. Speed is the differentiator. Delayed replies lose deals.

03

Run the Day-5 / Day-12 / Day-21 follow-up sequence every week. 60% of conversions happen on follow-ups, not the first email.

04

Log your numbers every Friday. One hour of review per week is non-negotiable.

05

Keep the queue above 50 at all times. If it drops below, stop everything and run a discovery batch first.
